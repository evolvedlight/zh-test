### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: Geographically, the Canton of Zurich belongs to the **Mittelland** (Swiss Plateau).

### The Plateau Landscape
The Mittelland is one of the three main geographical regions of Switzerland (alongside the Alps and the Jura).
*   **Topography:** It is characterized by hills, valleys, and lakes, with an average elevation between 400 and 700 meters.
*   **Climate:** The climate is relatively mild compared to the Alps, which made it the ideal place for the first settlements.
*   **Resources:** It has fertile soil for agriculture and a dense network of rivers.
