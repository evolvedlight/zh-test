### Local Education
While the Canton sets the curriculum and rules, the local municipality is responsible for:
*   **Infrastructure:** Building and maintaining school buildings.
*   **Staff:** Hiring teachers and school staff.
*   **Support:** Organizing school transport, lunches, and after-school care.
