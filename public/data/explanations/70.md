# ⚖️ The Executive Branch: The Federal Council

### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(a)**: On the federal level, the executive power (Exekutive) belongs to the **Federal Council** (Bundesrat).

### Tasks of the Executive
The Federal Council is the "Manager" of the country. Its duties include:
1.  **Enforcement:** Implementing the laws passed by the Parliament.
2.  **Administration:** Leading the seven departments of the federal administration (civil service).
3.  **Governance:** Proposing new laws and the annual budget to the Parliament.
4.  **Representation:** Representing Switzerland in foreign relations.

### Structure
The Executive is a **collective body** of seven equal members. There is no "Prime Minister" or "President" with superior power. Decisions are made by the council as a whole.
