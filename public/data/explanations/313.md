### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: The **Swiss National Museum** (Landesmuseum) in Zurich shows the **history of Switzerland**.

### A Journey through Time
Located right next to the Zurich Main Station (HB) in a building that looks like a fairytale castle, the Landesmuseum is the most visited history museum in the country.
*   **The Collection:** It houses the largest collection of cultural history in Switzerland, from prehistoric times to the 21st century.
*   **Permanent Exhibition:** "Simply Zurich" and "History of Switzerland" provide a deep look into the making of the nation, from the original cantons to modern democracy.
*   **Special Exhibitions:** It also hosts rotating shows on topics like Swiss design, art, and societal changes.

The museum is a must-visit for anyone who wants to understand the roots of Swiss identity and the development of the country's unique political system.
