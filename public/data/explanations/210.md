### A Powerful Leader
Alfred Escher was a politician and businessman from Zurich who dominated Swiss politics for decades.
1.  **Railway King:** He founded the North-Eastern Railway and led the construction of the **Gotthard Tunnel**, connecting the north and south of the Alps.
2.  **Education:** He was the driving force behind the creation of the **ETH Zurich** (Federal Institute of Technology) to train the engineers and specialists needed for the industrial age.
3.  **Finance:** He founded the **Schweizerische Kreditanstalt** (today known as Credit Suisse) to provide the capital for his massive industrial projects.

### Legacy
Escher is often called the "King of Switzerland" of his time. His work transformed Switzerland from a poor agricultural nation into a leading industrial and financial power. You can still see his statue in front of the Zurich Main Station (HB).
