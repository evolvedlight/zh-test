### The Executive Mandate
The Stadtrat is the "Government" of the city.
*   **Administration:** It oversees the thousands of employees working for the city (from trash collectors to police officers).
*   **Strategy:** It sets the long-term goals for the city's development (e.g., sustainability goals, housing projects, financial planning).
*   **Budgeting:** It proposes the annual budget to the City Parliament (Gemeinderat).
