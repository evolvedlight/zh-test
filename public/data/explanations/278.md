### A Diverse Society
Switzerland has one of the highest percentages of foreign residents in Europe (about 25-26% of the total population).
*   **Nationalities:** The largest groups come from Italy, Germany, Portugal, and France.
*   **Reasons:** Most come for work or family reunification.
*   **Impact:** This diversity is a major economic strength, as foreign workers fill essential roles in every sector, from construction and healthcare to high-tech research and finance.
*   **Naturalization:** Many of these 2.1 million people have lived in Switzerland for decades. To get the right to vote, they must go through the naturalization process (Einb√ºrgerung) to become Swiss citizens.
