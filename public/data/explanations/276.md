### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(a)**: **Zurich** is the largest city in Switzerland.

### Facts and Figures
*   **Population:** The City of Zurich has over **440,000 residents** (within city limits). The entire metropolitan area has more than 1.5 million people.
*   **Economy:** Zurich is the global center for **Banking and Finance** and home to many international companies and the ETH (university).
*   **Transport:** It has the country's largest airport (Kloten) and the busiest railway station (HB).
*   **Culture:** It offers a world-class opera house, many museums (like the Kunsthaus), and a vibrant nightlife.

### Other Cities
*   **Geneva (d):** Second largest, famous for international organizations (UN, Red Cross).
*   **Basel (b):** Third largest, center of the pharmaceutical industry.
*   **Bern (c):** Fourth largest, and the **federal capital** (Bundesstadt) where the government and parliament are located.
