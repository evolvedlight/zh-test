# ⛷️ Winter Sports: Skiing

### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(a)**: Many people in Switzerland go **skiing** (Skifahren) in the winter.

### A National Pastime
Skiing is much more than just a sport in Switzerland; it is a major part of the cultural identity and the economy.
*   **Widespread:** A huge percentage of the Swiss population knows how to ski. Most children learn it at a very young age, often through mandatory "Ski Camps" organized by schools.
*   **Geography:** The Alps and the Jura offer hundreds of world-class ski resorts.
*   **Economy:** Winter tourism is a vital source of income for many mountain cantons (like Valais, Graubünden, and Bern).
*   **National Pride:** Successful Swiss skiers (like Marco Odermatt or Lara Gut-Behrami) are celebrated as national heroes.
