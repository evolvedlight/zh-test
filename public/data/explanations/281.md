### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(d)**: As of 2020, about **8.6 million** people lived in Switzerland.

### Growth and Trends
The Swiss population has been growing steadily over the last decades.
*   **Today (2024):** The population has already passed the **9 million** mark.
*   **Reasons:** The growth is mainly driven by immigration (Zuwanderung) from other European countries and an increase in life expectancy.
*   **Urbanization:** Most people live in the metropolitan areas of Zurich, Geneva, Basel, Lausanne, and Bern.
*   **Aging:** Like most Western countries, Switzerland has an aging population, which puts pressure on the social security and healthcare systems.

Knowing the size of the population is important for understanding the scale of the economy, the density of the infrastructure, and the weight of the "Sovereign" in democratic decisions.
