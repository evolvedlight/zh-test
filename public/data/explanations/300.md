### A Historic Landmark
Located near Winterthur, Schloss Kyburg is one of the most important and best-preserved medieval castles in Eastern Switzerland.
*   **Significance:** It was the seat of the powerful Counts of Kyburg and later used by the City of Zurich to govern its subject territories.
*   **Museum:** Today, it is an award-winning museum that shows how life was in a castle hundreds of years ago.
