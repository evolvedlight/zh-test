# ✈️ The Home of the Airport: Kloten

### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: Zurich Airport (ZRH) is located in the municipality of **Kloten**.

### Facts about the Airport
While everyone calls it "Zurich Airport," the actual tarmac and terminals are located about 10 kilometers north of the city center in Kloten.
*   **Size:** It is Switzerland's largest international gateway and a major hub for Swiss International Air Lines.
*   **Economy:** The airport is one of the largest employers in the canton, providing jobs for over 25,000 people.
*   **Transport:** It is perfectly connected to the city of Zurich by train (S-Bahn) and tram, taking only about 10-15 minutes.

### Other Municipalities
*   **Bülach (d):** Is a neighboring town but does not host the airport itself.
*   **Winterthur (b):** Is the second-largest city in the canton, located further to the northeast.
*   **Zurich (a):** The city owns a large part of the airport company, but the land is in Kloten.
