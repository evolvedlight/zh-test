### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: One of the most important fundamental rights in Switzerland is the **freedom of opinion and information** (Meinungs- und Informationsfreiheit).

### What it Means
*   **Article 16 of the Constitution:** Every person has the right to form, express, and spread their opinions freely.
*   **Media Freedom:** The state cannot censor newspapers, TV, or radio. Journalists can report critically on the government.
*   **Right to Access:** You can gather information from generally accessible sources without interference.

### Limits
Like all rights, this is not absolute. You cannot:
*   Incite hatred or violence (racism, discrimination).
*   Libel or slander others (damaging their reputation with lies).
*   Reveal state secrets.
