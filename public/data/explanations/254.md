### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: **Huldrych Zwingli** reformed the church in Zurich.

### The Zurich Reformation
While Martin Luther (c) was the main reformer in Germany, and Jean Calvin (d) was later the leader in Geneva, Zurich's own reformation was led by Zwingli.
*   **The Disputations:** Zwingli convinced the Zurich City Council to support his ideas through a series of public debates (Disputations) in the 1520s.
*   **A New Order:** The Catholic Mass was abolished and replaced by a simpler Protestant service.
*   **Social Reform:** The property of the monasteries was taken over by the city and used to fund schools and social welfare for the poor.

### Significance
Zwingli's reformation made Zurich a lighthouse of the Protestant faith in Europe and deeply shaped the culture, education system, and work ethic of the region for centuries to come.
