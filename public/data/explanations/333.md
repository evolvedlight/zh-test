### Geneva: The Humanitarian Capital
Switzerland hosts many international organizations, most of them in the "International City" of Geneva.
*   **The ICRC:** Founded in Geneva in 1863 by Henry Dunant. It is a purely Swiss organization that acts internationally to protect victims of war and ensure humanitarian standards (Geneva Conventions).
*   **Other Organizations in Switzerland:** 
    *   United Nations (UN / UNO) - Second headquarters.
    *   World Health Organization (WHO).
    *   World Trade Organization (WTO).
    *   FIFA (in Zurich).
    *   IOC (International Olympic Committee in Lausanne).

### Why Switzerland?
The tradition of **Neutrality** and the reputation for safety, reliability, and excellent infrastructure make Switzerland the ideal location for organizations that need to work with all countries, even those at war.
