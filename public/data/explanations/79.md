### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(d)**: The freedom of belief and conscience guarantees **religious freedom** (Religionsfreiheit).

### Key Aspects
1.  **Positive Freedom:** The right to have a religion, to express it, and to join religious groups.
2.  **Negative Freedom:** The right *not* to have a religion and the right not to be forced to participate in religious acts.
3.  **Neutrality:** The state should be neutral towards different religions and worldviews.

### Education
This right also means that parents have the right to decide on the religious education of their children until the age of **16**. After 16, a teenager can decide for themselves which religion (if any) they want to follow.
*   **Worship:** It includes the right to wear religious symbols (like a cross or headscarf) in public, as long as it doesn't interfere with public safety or specific professional requirements.
