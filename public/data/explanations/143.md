### Partnership
In Switzerland, unions and employers usually follow the principle of **"Social Partnership"** (Sozialpartnerschaft). This means they try to find compromises through negotiation rather than going on strike immediately. This leads to a very stable labor market with very few strikes compared to other European countries.
