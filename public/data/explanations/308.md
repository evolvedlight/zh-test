### Culture
Schwingen is deeply rooted in rural and Alpine traditions. It is celebrated at festivals accompanied by Alphorn music, stone-throwing (Unspunnenstein), and yodeling. Today, it is experiencing a massive revival and draws huge crowds and TV audiences across the country.
