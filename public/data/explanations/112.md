### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: The parliament (Legislative) of the Canton of Zurich is called the **Cantonsrat**.

### Facts about the Cantonsrat
*   **Size:** It has **180 members**.
*   **Tasks:** Its main jobs are passing cantonal laws and approving the annual budget and accounts of the canton.
*   **Election:** Members are elected by the people using proportional representation every 4 years.
*   **Meeting Place:** They meet in the Rathaus (Town Hall) in Zurich, a historic building on the Limmat river.

### Other Names
*   **Grosser Rat (b):** This name is used for the parliaments in many other cantons (like Bern, Aargau, or Basel).
*   **Landtag (a):** This term is used in Germany or Liechtenstein, but never in a Swiss canton.
*   **Regierungsrat (d):** This is the name of the *Executive* in Zurich, not the parliament.
