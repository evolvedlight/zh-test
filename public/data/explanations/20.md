This is a federal compromise:
*   **Nationalrat:** Seats are distributed by population. Zurich has 36 seats, Uri has 1.
*   **St√§nderat:** Seats are distributed by canton. Zurich has 2, Uri has 2. This prevents the large cantons from ignoring the small ones.

### The Legislative Process
For a law to pass, **both** chambers must agree on the exact same text. If they disagree, they negotiate back and forth (Differenzbereinigung) until a compromise is found or the bill fails.
