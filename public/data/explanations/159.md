### When to call 144?
You should call this number in any life-threatening situation or when a person needs urgent medical attention, such as:
*   Heart attack or stroke symptoms.
*   Severe injuries after an accident.
*   Loss of consciousness.
*   Heavy bleeding or severe breathing difficulties.

### Professional Help
The operators are trained medical professionals who can give instructions over the phone (e.g., for CPR) while the ambulance is on its way. In Switzerland, calling an ambulance is expensive, but the costs are covered by health or accident insurance (minus the franchise/deductible).
