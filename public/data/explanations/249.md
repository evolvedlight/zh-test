### Roman Lifestyle
When the Romans settled in Zurich, they brought their Mediterranean lifestyle with them.
*   **Public Baths:** In Turicum (modern-day Zurich), the Romans built public thermal baths. Bathing was not just for hygiene; it was a central part of social lifeâ€”a place to meet friends, discuss business, and relax.
*   **Thermengasse:** You can still see the remains of these Roman baths today in the **Thermengasse**, a small alleyway in the heart of Zurich's Old Town near the Lindenhof.
*   **Infrastructure:** Besides baths, they also introduced viticulture (wine-making), fruit trees like cherries and plums, and stone masonry.
