### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: Switzerland celebrates its national holiday on **August 1st**.

### Tradition and Meaning
*   **1291:** The date commemorates the traditional founding of the Swiss Confederacy when the first three cantons (Uri, Schwyz, Unterwalden) swore an oath of alliance (the Bundesbrief).
*   **Celebrations:** It is a day of public festivities. Typical celebrations include:
    *   Speeches by local and national politicians.
    *   Large bonfires on mountain peaks and in villages.
    *   Fireworks in many cities.
    *   Lantern parades for children.
*   **Holiday:** Since 1994, it has been an official work-free day throughout the entire country.
