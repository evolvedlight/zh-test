### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(a)**: In the City of Zurich, the executive branch (government) is called the **Stadtrat**.

### Hierarchy of Names
Names of authorities change depending on the level of government:
1.  **Federal (Bund):** Bundesrat (7 members).
2.  **Cantonal (Canton Zurich):** Regierungsrat (7 members).
3.  **Municipal (City of Zurich):** Stadtrat (9 members).

### Tasks of the Stadtrat
The Stadtrat manages the city's specific needs, such as:
*   City planning and housing.
*   City police and fire department.
*   Waste disposal and city parks.
*   Social services and local schools.

### The Legislative Counterpart
The "Parliament" of the City of Zurich is called the **Gemeinderat** (125 members). Note that in small villages, the "Gemeinderat" is often the name of the *Executive*, but in large cities like Zurich, it is the name of the *Legislative*.
