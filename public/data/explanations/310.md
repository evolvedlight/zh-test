### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(a)**: SBB stands for **Schweizerische Bundesbahnen** (Swiss Federal Railways).

### The Backbone of Transport
The SBB is the most important transport company in Switzerland.
*   **Trilingual Name:** Reflecting Switzerland's languages, it has three official names: **SBB** (German), **CFF** (French), and **FFS** (Italian).
*   **History:** It was created in 1902 when several private railway companies were merged and nationalized.
*   **Performance:** Every day, over 1 million people travel on SBB trains. It is famous worldwide for its punctuality, cleanliness, and the "Taktfahrplan" (regular interval schedule).
*   **Cargo:** SBB Cargo is also a major player in European freight transport, especially across the Alps.

### Ownership
The SBB is a public limited company (Aktiengesellschaft), but 100% of the shares are owned by the **Swiss Confederation**. Its goal is to provide a vital public service, not just to maximize profits.
