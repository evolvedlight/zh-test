### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: MWST stands for **Mehrwertsteuer** (Value Added Tax / VAT).

### How it Works
It is a "consumption tax" collected by the Confederation (Bund).
*   **Payment:** Every time you buy something (a coffee, a phone, a train ticket), a small percentage is included in the price.
*   **Collection:** The business collects the tax from you and sends it to the Federal Tax Administration.
*   **Rates:** Switzerland has one of the lowest VAT rates in Europe.
    *   **Standard Rate (8.1%):** For most goods and services.
    *   **Reduced Rate (2.6%):** For essential goods like food, medicine, books, and newspapers.
    *   **Special Rate (3.8%):** For the hotel industry.

### Importance
The MWST is the most important source of income for the Swiss Confederation, financing everything from the army to national highways.
