### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(d)**: To yodel, you only need your **voice** (Stimme).

### The Technique
Yodeling is a specialized vocal technique. It requires the singer to quickly switch between two different vocal registers:
1.  **Chest Register (Bruststimme):** For the low, powerful notes.
2.  **Head Register (Kopfstimme/Fistelstimme):** For the high, light notes.
