### The Collapse of the "Old Zurich"

*   **The Helvetic Republic:** The old system collapsed. The French established the "Helvetic Republic," a centralized state where **all residents were equal** for the first time.
*   **Legacy:** Although the Helvetic Republic failed and was replaced in 1803, the "spirit of 1798" introduced the modern ideas of **individual liberty** and **equality** that would later become the foundation of the 1848 Constitution.
