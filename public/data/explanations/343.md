### The World's Largest Techno Party
Held every August along the lake shore of Zurich, the Street Parade is a massive electronic music festival.
*   **Origin:** Founded in 1992 as a "Demonstration for Love, Peace, Freedom, Generosity, and Tolerance."
*   **Scale:** It attracts around **1 million people** from all over the world, making it the largest parade of its kind.
*   **Love Mobiles:** Dozens of trucks (Love Mobiles) carrying DJs and sound systems drive slowly through the crowds.
*   **Economic Impact:** It is a major event for the Zurich tourism and hospitality industry.

### Philosophy
Despite its size and loud music, the Street Parade is known for its peaceful atmosphere. It remains a non-profit event organized by a local association, emphasizing that in Zurich, everyone can be themselves and celebrate together regardless of their background.
