### Cantonal Differences
Holidays like Ascension (b), Good Friday (c), or May 1st (d) are holidays in *many* cantons, but not all. For example, some Catholic cantons have different holidays than Protestant ones. Only the 1st of August is a day off for everyone in Switzerland by federal law.
