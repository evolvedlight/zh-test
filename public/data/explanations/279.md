### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: The mountain shown in the picture is the **Matterhorn**.

### Facts about the Matterhorn
*   **Shape:** It is world-famous for its near-perfect **pyramid shape**.
*   **Location:** It is located in the **Canton of Valais**, near the village of **Zermatt**, on the border with Italy.
*   **Height:** 4,478 meters above sea level.
*   **Symbolism:** It is the most famous landmark of Switzerland and a global icon for the Alps. You can see its shape on many Swiss products, most famously on **Toblerone** chocolate.
*   **Climbing:** It was first climbed in 1865, a historic and tragic event that made the mountain famous.
