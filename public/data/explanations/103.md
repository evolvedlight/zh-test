The correct answer is **(b)**: A central task of the Canton of Zurich is the **organization of the University**.

### Higher Education
While the federal government (Bund) manages the ETH (Federal Institute of Technology), the **University of Zurich (UZH)** is a cantonal institution.
*   **Funding:** The canton provides the majority of the funding and sets the legal framework for the university.
*   **Education System:** Cantons are generally responsible for the entire school system, from kindergarten and primary school to high schools (Gymnasien) and vocational schools.

### What the Canton does NOT do:
*   **Foreign Policy (a):** Only the Bund can sign treaties with the EU.
*   **Digitalization of Bund (c):** The federal administration manages its own digital transition.
*   **The Army (d):** The military is a purely federal responsibility.
