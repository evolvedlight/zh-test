### Taxing Assets
In Switzerland, you don't just pay tax on what you *earn*, but also on what you *have*.
*   **What is taxed?** Bank balances, stocks, bonds, real estate, cars, and other valuables.
*   **Calculation:** You subtract your debts (like a mortgage) from your total assets. The remaining "Net Wealth" is taxed.
*   **Rate:** The rate is usually very low (a few per mille).
*   **Importance:** It is a key source of income for the Cantons and Municipalities, helping them fund schools and infrastructure.
