### Geography of Zurich
Zurich is located in the northeastern part of the Swiss plateau (Mittelland).
*   **Neighbors:** It is bordered by the cantons of Schaffhausen (North), Aargau (West), Zug and Schwyz (South), and Thurgau and St. Gallen (East).
*   **National Border:** It has a small international border with **Germany** in the north.
*   **Landscape:** The canton is characterized by its rolling hills and large lakes (Lake Zurich, Greifensee).

### Importance of Location
Zurich's central location in northeastern Switzerland has made it the country's most important transport and economic hub. It serves as the gateway between the German-speaking interior and the international markets to the north.
