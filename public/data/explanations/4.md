The Swiss federal government (Bundesrat) has 7 members. Each member is the head of one of the 7 Federal Departments (Ministries). The departments are:

1. **EDA**: Foreign Affairs
2. **EDI**: Home Affairs (Interior)
3. **EJPD**: Justice and Police
4. **VBS**: Defence, Civil Protection and Sport
5. **EFD**: Finance
6. **WBF**: Economic Affairs, Education and Research
7. **UVEK**: Environment, Transport, Energy and Communications

The "Justiz- und Polizeidepartement" (Justice and Police) is the correct answer. The other options listed are not real federal departments.
