### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(d)**: The capital city (Hauptstadt) of the Canton of Zurich is **Zurich**.

### The Role of the Capital
As the capital, the City of Zurich is the political and administrative heart of the canton.
*   **Government Seat:** The **Regierungsrat** (Cantonal Government) has its offices here.
*   **Parliament Seat:** The **Cantonsrat** (Cantonal Parliament) meets in the Rathaus (Town Hall) in Zurich's Old Town.
*   **Administration:** The main offices of the seven cantonal directorates (departments) are located in the city.

### Other Major Cities
While Winterthur (b) and Uster (a) are large and important cities in their own right, the City of Zurich holds the title of capital. It is both the largest city in the canton and the largest in all of Switzerland.
