### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: The National Council and the Council of States meet jointly (United Federal Assembly) to **elect members of the Federal Council and the Federal Supreme Court**.

### The Election Day
Elections for the Federal Council usually take place every four years in December, following the national elections.
1.  **United Federal Assembly:** All 246 members sit together in the National Council Chamber.
2.  **Federal Council:** They elect the seven ministers individually.
3.  **Chancellor:** They also elect the Federal Chancellor.
4.  **Judges:** They elect judges for the various federal courts (located in Lausanne, Lucerne, Bellinzona, and St. Gallen).

### Other Joint Powers
They also meet to decide on **Pardons** (Gnadengesuche) and to resolve jurisdictional disputes between the highest federal authorities.
