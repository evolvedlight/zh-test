### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(a)**: The "United Federal Assembly" (Vereinigte Bundesversammlung) is the meeting of **all members of the National Council and the Council of States** in one hall.

### When do they meet?
They usually meet four times a year (during sessions) in the National Council Chamber for special occasions:
1.  **Elections:** Electing members of the Federal Council, the Federal Chancellor, and judges of the Federal Supreme Court.
2.  **Pardons:** Deciding on petitions for pardon.
3.  **Conflicts:** Resolving jurisdictional conflicts between federal authorities.
4.  **Special Speeches:** Hearing the annual speech of the President or visiting foreign heads of state.

### The Presidency
When they meet together, the President of the **National Council** chairs the meeting. This reflects the National Council's status as the chamber representing the people directly.
