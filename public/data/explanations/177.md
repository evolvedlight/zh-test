### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(d)**: You can find information about apprenticeships and careers at the **Berufsinformationszentrum** (BIZ).

### What the BIZ offers
The BIZ is a cantonal service (part of the Bildungsdirektion in Zurich) that helps young people and adults with their career choices.
*   **Information:** They have huge libraries of brochures, videos, and online tools about every possible job in Switzerland.
*   **Counseling:** You can talk to a "Berufsberater" (Career Counselor) for free. They help you find your strengths and suggest matching professions.
*   **Apprenticeships:** They maintain lists of open "Lehrstellen" (apprenticeship positions) in the region.
*   **Adults:** The BIZ also helps adults who want to change their career (Laufbahnberatung) or pursue further education.

In the Canton of Zurich, there are several BIZ locations, for example in Zurich city, Winterthur, Uster, and Kloten.
