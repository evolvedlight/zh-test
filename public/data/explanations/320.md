### Career and Legacy
Born in Basel in 1981, Roger Federer is widely considered one of the greatest athletes of all time.
*   **Success:** He won 20 Grand Slam titles and spent a record 310 weeks as world number one.
*   **Role Model:** Beyond his athletic success, he is admired for his sportsmanship, grace, and humility. He is a "perfect ambassador" for Swiss values worldwide.
*   **Charity:** Through his foundation, he supports educational projects for children, especially in Southern Africa and Switzerland.

### Swiss Identity
For many people around the world, Roger Federer *is* the face of modern Switzerland. His career has contributed significantly to the positive image of the country as a place of precision, reliability, and excellence.
