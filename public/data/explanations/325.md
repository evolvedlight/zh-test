### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: The Canton of **Zurich** has the most residents in Switzerland.

### Demographic Weight
*   **Population:** With over **1.5 million** people, Zurich is significantly larger than any other canton.
*   **Political Power:** In the Swiss system, seats in the National Council are distributed based on population. Because Zurich has the most residents, it sends the largest delegation (**36 members**) to the federal parliament in Bern.
*   **Economic Hub:** Zurich's high population is both a result and a driver of its strong economy, as it attracts workers and companies from across Switzerland and the world.

*   **Bern (b):** Is the second most populous canton (around 1 million).
