### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(a)**: In the Canton of Zurich, compulsory education begins with **Kindergarten**.

### The Path
*   **Entry:** Children usually enter Kindergarten at the age of 4 (the child must be 4 by the 31st of July to start in August).
*   **Duration:** Kindergarten lasts for **2 years**. It is a mandatory part of the "Volksschule" (public school system).
*   **Goal:** The focus is on social learning, language development, and preparing children for the more structured environment of the primary school.

### HarmoS Agreement
Zurich follows the Swiss-wide "HarmoS" agreement, which harmonized the starting age and duration of compulsory education across most cantons.
