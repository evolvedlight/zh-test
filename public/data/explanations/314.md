### The Watch Valley (Arc Jurassien)
The arc of the Jura mountains, stretching from Geneva to Basel, is the heart of global luxury watchmaking.
*   **Precision:** Known as the "Watch Valley," it is home to most of the famous Swiss watch brands (e.g., Longines, Tissot, Jaeger-LeCoultre).
*   **History:** The industry grew in the 18th century. Farmers in the Jura valleys began making watch parts during the long, snow-bound winter months to earn extra money.
*   **Expertise:** This developed into a culture of extreme precision and high-tech engineering that remains a pillar of the Swiss economy today.

While the Jura also has agriculture and some textile history, its global reputation is built entirely on the mastery of time.
