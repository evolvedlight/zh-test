# ✍️ Great Swiss Writers: Frisch and Dürrenmatt

### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: Max Frisch and Friedrich Dürrenmatt were famous Swiss **writers** (Schriftsteller).

### The Giants of Swiss Literature
Frisch and Dürrenmatt are the most important Swiss authors of the 20th century. Their work is known far beyond the borders of Switzerland.
1.  **Max Frisch (1911–1991):** A Zurich architect and writer. Famous for novels like *Stiller* and *Homo Faber*, and plays like *Andorra*. He often explored themes of identity, guilt, and the role of Switzerland in the world.
2.  **Friedrich Dürrenmatt (1921–1990):** From the Canton of Bern. Famous for satirical and philosophical plays like *The Visit* (Der Besuch der alten Dame) and *The Physicists* (Die Physiker), as well as detective novels.

### Cultural Significance
Both writers were critical observers of Swiss society and politics. Their works are mandatory reading in Swiss schools and are performed on theater stages worldwide, representing the intellectual depth and critical spirit of Swiss culture.
