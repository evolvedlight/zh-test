### The "Oath of Alliance"
The word comes from "Eid" (oath) and "Genossenschaft" (fellowship/partnership).
*   **Origin:** It refers to the legendary pact of 1291, where the representatives of the three original cantons swore an oath to support each other against foreign rulers.
*   **Official Name:** In German, the full name of the country is *Schweizerische Eidgenossenschaft*.

### Meaning Today
The term emphasizes that Switzerland is not a country created by a king or a single ethnic group, but a voluntary union of diverse partners (Cantons) who have agreed to live together under common rules.
