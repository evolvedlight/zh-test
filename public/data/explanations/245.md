### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: Switzerland has been a world leader in the **Watch Industry** (Uhrenindustrie) for centuries.

### A History of Quality
*   **Origins:** The industry grew in the 16th century, especially in Geneva and the Jura mountains, driven by Protestant refugees (Huguenots) who brought their watchmaking skills from France.
*   **Reputation:** The "Swiss Made" label became a worldwide symbol of precision, luxury, and reliability.
*   **The Quartz Crisis:** In the 1970s, the industry almost collapsed due to cheap quartz watches from Asia, but it reinvented itself with brands like **Swatch** and by focusing on high-end mechanical watches.
*   **Global Export:** Today, Switzerland is the world's largest exporter of watches by value, with brands like Rolex, Omega, and Patek Philippe being famous in every corner of the globe.
