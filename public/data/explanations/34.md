### Stability and Predictability
In many countries, the government can call "snap elections" if they lose a majority. In Switzerland, this is **not possible**. The 4-year term is fixed. This provides significant political stability. If a Federal Councillor (minister) resigns mid-term, they are replaced by the Parliament, but the Parliament itself stays until the next 4-year mark.
