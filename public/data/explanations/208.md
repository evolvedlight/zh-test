### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: The **Schulleitung** (School Management) is responsible for the operational management of public schools in Zurich municipalities.

### Professional Management
In the past, schools were often managed by the teachers themselves or a lay board of citizens (Schulpflege). Today, every school has a professional principal (Schulleiter/in).
*   **Tasks:** They handle the daily organization, the deployment of teachers, pedagogical quality, and communication with parents.
*   **Accountability:** The Schulleitung reports to the local school board (Schulpflege) and the cantonal education directorate.
*   **Elternrat (a):** The parents' council has a consultative role and helps with school projects, but they have no power to "lead" the school or make management decisions.
