### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: The Gotthard Base Tunnel is the **longest railway tunnel in the world**.

### A Masterpiece of Engineering
Opened in **2016**, the tunnel is a central part of the NRLA (New Rail Link through the Alps) project.
*   **Length:** It is **57 kilometers** long.
*   **Location:** It runs from Erstfeld (Uri) to Bodio (Ticino), straight through the heart of the Alps.
*   **"Base" Tunnel:** Unlike the old mountain tunnel which climbed high up the slopes, the base tunnel is at the "base" of the mountain (at the level of the valley). This makes it flat and fast.
*   **Purpose:** To shift the transport of heavy goods from trucks on the roads to trains on the tracks, protecting the Alpine environment and reducing travel time for passengers between Zurich and Milan to under 3 hours.

### Global Significance
For Switzerland, this tunnel was a project of the century, demonstrating the country's innovative power and its role as a "Transit Country" at the center of Europe.
