### Music of the Alps
Yodeling is a form of singing that involves rapid changes of pitch between the low-pitched chest voice and the high-pitched falsetto.
*   **Origin:** It originated in the Alps as a way for herders to communicate across valleys or to call their cattle.
*   **Types:** 
    *   **Naturjodel:** Without words, focusing on the melody and the echo.
    *   **Jodellied:** Songs with lyrics and a yodeled chorus.
