### The "Fifth Season"
Fasnacht is celebrated in late winter (February/March) to drive away the spirits of winter and welcome spring. It is a time when traditional social rules are turned upside down.
*   **Masks and Costumes:** People dress up in elaborate, often scary or satirical costumes and wear masks (Larven).
*   **Guggenmusik:** Loud, brass-band music that is intentionally "out of tune" but very energetic.
*   **Regional Differences:**
    *   **Basel:** Famous for the "Morgestraich" and its high-quality drums and piccolos.
    *   **Lucerne:** Known for its massive, creative parades.
    *   **Zurich:** Has its own smaller celebrations, but is not as famous for Fasnacht as Basel or Lucerne.

Fasnacht is a vibrant part of Swiss cultural life, blending ancient pagan rituals with medieval Christian traditions and modern political satire.
