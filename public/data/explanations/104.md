### What is a District?
A district is an administrative level between the Municipality and the Canton. They do not have their own parliament or government, but they serve as:
1.  **Judicial Areas:** Each district has its own District Court (Bezirksgericht).
2.  **Administrative Seats:** Each district has a "Statthalter" (a representative of the cantonal government) and a "Bezirksrat."
3.  **Notary/Bankruptcy Offices:** These services are often organized at the district level.
