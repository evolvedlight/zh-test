# ⚽ Most Popular Club Sport: Football

### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(d)**: **Football** (Fussball) is the sport most frequently practiced in a club (Verein) in Switzerland.

### A Nation of Players
While Switzerland is famous for its mountains, football is the undisputed number one popular sport.
*   **Participation:** There are over 1,400 football clubs in Switzerland with nearly 300,000 active players (including many children and women).
*   **National Team:** The "Nati" (Swiss National Team) is extremely popular and consistently ranks among the top teams in the world.
*   **Clubs in Zurich:** Zurich is home to two major professional clubs, **FC Zürich** and **Grasshopper Club Zürich**, as well as dozens of amateur clubs in every neighborhood.

### Other Sports
*   **Unihockey (c):** (Floorball) is very popular, especially among young people, and is often cited as the second-most common team sport.
*   **Skiing (b):** While more people might *go* skiing, they usually do it individually or with family rather than being a member of a "ski club" for regular practice.
*   **Swimming (a):** Is a popular leisure activity but has fewer active club members than football.
