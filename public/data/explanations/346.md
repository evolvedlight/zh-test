### The Science Center
The **Swiss Science Center Technorama** is one of the largest and most interactive science museums in Europe.
*   **Philosophy:** "Hands-on" is the golden rule. Instead of just looking at exhibits, visitors are encouraged to touch, try out, and experiment themselves.
*   **Exhibits:** It covers physics, chemistry, biology, and technology through over 500 experimental stations.
*   **Target Audience:** It is a favorite destination for families, school classes, and anyone interested in understanding the laws of nature through play and discovery.
*   **Regional Pride:** Winterthur is proud to host such a world-class institution, which reflects the city's long history as a center of technical engineering and industry.
