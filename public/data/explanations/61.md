### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: The **Confederation (Bund)** is responsible for **foreign policy**.

### Scope of Federal Power
As the highest level of government, the Bund handles all relations with other countries and international organizations (like the UN or the EU). This ensures that Switzerland speaks with **one voice** on the world stage.
*   **Treaties:** The Bund negotiates and signs international agreements.
*   **Neutrality:** The Bund manages Switzerland's neutral status.
*   **Aid:** The Bund (via the SDC/DEZA) provides international humanitarian aid and development cooperation.

### What the Cantons do:
*   **Education (a):** Schools and kindergartens are managed by cantons and municipalities.
*   **Building Codes (c):** Zoning laws and construction permits are cantonal or communal matters.
*   **Judiciary (d):** While there are federal courts, cantonal judges are elected or appointed by the **Cantons** themselves.
