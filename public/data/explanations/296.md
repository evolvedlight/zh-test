### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(d)**: The **Greifensee** is the second largest lake in the Canton of Zurich.

### Nature and Recreation
*   **Location:** It lies in the Zurich Oberland, east of Zurich city. The largest town on its shore is Uster.
*   **Protection:** The entire shoreline of the Greifensee is a nature preserve. Unlike Lake Zurich, there are very few private gardens directly on the water, making it a paradise for birds and walkers.
*   **Size:** It is about 6 kilometers long and 1.6 kilometers wide.

### Other options:
*   **Walensee (a) / Zugersee (b):** These are large lakes but are located outside (or only partially bordering) the Canton of Zurich.
*   **Sihlsee (c):** A large artificial lake in the **Canton of Schwyz**.
