### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: An essential task of the Confederation (Bund) is ensuring **internal and external security**.

### External Security
*   **Neutrality:** Maintaining a position of non-involvement in foreign wars.
*   **Military:** Maintaining the Swiss Army (Milizarmee) to defend the territory if necessary.
*   **Diplomacy:** Fostering good relations with other countries to prevent conflict.

### Internal Security
*   **Federal Police (fedpol):** Coordinating with cantonal police on serious crimes (terrorism, organized crime).
*   **Intelligence Service (NDB):** Monitoring threats to the state.
*   **Crisis Management:** Planning for pandemics, blackouts, or natural disasters.

### What the Bund does NOT do:
*   **Hospitals (d):** Running hospitals is primarily the responsibility of the **Cantons**.
*   **Company Founding (b):** Founding companies is a private matter. The state only provides the legal framework.
