### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: The headquarters of **FIFA** (the world football governing body) is located in Zurich.

### Home of Football
FIFA was founded in Paris in 1904 but moved its headquarters to Zurich in 1932.
*   **The Building:** The "Home of FIFA" is a modern office complex located near the Zurich Zoo.
*   **Significance:** FIFA organizes the World Cup and governs international football for over 200 member nations.
*   **Local Impact:** Together with many other sports organizations, FIFA makes Zurich an important center for international sports management.

### Other Sports HQ in Switzerland
Switzerland is a magnet for sports federations due to its favorable legal and tax environment:
*   **FIS (Skiing - b):** Headquartered in Oberhofen (Canton of Bern).
*   **IOC (Olympics):** Headquartered in **Lausanne** (known as the "Olympic Capital").
*   **UEFA (Football Europe):** Headquartered in Nyon (Vaud).
