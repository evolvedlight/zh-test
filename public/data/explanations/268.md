### Life in the Mountains
Being a Bergkanton brings specific challenges and opportunities:
1.  **Tourism:** Winter sports and summer hiking are the main income sources.
2.  **Hydropower:** The steep valleys are perfect for large dams and electricity production.
3.  **Infrastructure:** Maintaining roads and railways in avalanche and landslide zones is very expensive.
