# ⚖️ The UNO Human Rights Council in Geneva

### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(c)**: The UN Human Rights Council (formerly Commission) is based in **Geneva**.

### Geneva: Center of Human Rights
While New York is the political headquarters of the United Nations, Geneva is the operational and humanitarian heart.
*   **Palais des Nations:** The second largest UN seat in the world.
*   **OHCHR:** The Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Human Rights is based in the "Palais Wilson" in Geneva.
*   **The Council:** The UN Human Rights Council meets several times a year in Geneva to discuss the human rights situation in all countries.

### Significance
Having these institutions in Switzerland reinforces the country's humanitarian tradition and its commitment to international law. It also brings thousands of diplomats and experts to the country, making Geneva one of the most international cities in the world.
