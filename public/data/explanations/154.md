### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(d)**: IV stands for **Invalidenversicherung** (Disability Insurance).

### Scope
It is a mandatory national insurance. Its purpose is to guarantee the livelihood of persons who become disabled.
*   **Disability:** A person is considered disabled if their earning capacity is permanently or long-term impaired due to a health issue (physical or mental).
*   **Early Detection:** The IV also works on "early intervention" to prevent a person from having to leave their job in the first place when health problems arise.
*   **Aids:** It also pays for aids like wheelchairs or hearing aids if they are necessary for the person to work or manage their daily life.
