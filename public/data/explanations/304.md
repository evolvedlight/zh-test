### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: The Canton of **Thurgau** borders the Canton of Zurich.

### Neighboring Cantons
Zurich is surrounded by six other cantons:
1.  **Thurgau:** To the northeast.
2.  **Schaffhausen:** To the north.
3.  **Aargau:** To the west.
4.  **Zug:** To the southwest.
5.  **Schwyz:** To the south.
6.  **St. Gallen:** To the east and southeast.

### Why not the others?
*   **Freiburg (a) / Luzern (c):** Are located in Western and Central Switzerland, respectively, and do not share a border with Zurich.
*   **Glarus (d):** Is close to the southern border of St. Gallen and Schwyz but does not directly touch the Canton of Zurich.
