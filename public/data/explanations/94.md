### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(a)**: In Switzerland, the **Obligationenrecht (Code of Obligations)** regulates the **renting of apartments**.

### What is the OR?
The OR is the fifth part of the Swiss Civil Code. It covers all types of contracts and obligations between people or companies.
*   **Rental Contracts (Mietrecht):** Articles 253 to 273c of the OR define the rights and duties of landlords and tenants (e.g., notice periods, rent increases, deposits, and repairs).
*   **Labor Law:** It also regulates employment contracts.

### Other Codes:
*   **StGB (c):** Deals with crimes (theft, assault).
*   **MWSTG (b):** Deals with Value Added Tax.
*   **SchKG (d):** Deals with debt collection and bankruptcy (if you don't pay your bills).
