### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(a)**: The cantons are primarily responsible for **managing the cantonal administration and passing cantonal laws**.

### Cantonal Sovereignty
In the Swiss federal system, cantons are sovereign in all areas where the Constitution does not assign power to the Confederation.
*   **Education:** From primary school to University.
*   **Police:** Maintaining public order and safety within the canton.
*   **Healthcare:** Planning and funding hospitals.
*   **Taxes:** Setting and collecting cantonal taxes.

### What they do NOT do:
*   **National Defense (c):** The military is a task for the Bund.
*   **Foreign Policy (b):** Relations with other countries are handled by the Bund.
*   **Money and Customs (d):** The Bund manages the currency (SNB) and the borders.
