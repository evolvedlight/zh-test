### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(a)**: As of 2020, about **1.5 million** people lived in the Canton of Zurich.

### The Most Populous Canton
Zurich is the most populous canton in Switzerland, containing about 18% of the total Swiss population.
*   **Density:** It is also one of the most densely populated cantons, with most people living in the metropolitan areas around the City of Zurich and Winterthur.
*   **Growth:** The population continues to grow, driven by the strong economy and the attractiveness of the region for international workers and students.
*   **Diversity:** About one-third of the residents are foreigners, making Zurich a very international and multicultural place.

Understanding the size of the population is key to understanding the weight of Zurich in federal politics (sending the most representatives to the National Council) and its role as the economic engine of the country.
