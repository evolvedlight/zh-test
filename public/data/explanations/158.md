### What it does
The BAG is part of the Federal Department of Home Affairs (EDI). Its main tasks are:
*   **Health Policy:** Developing the national health policy.
*   **Insurance Oversight:** Supervising the mandatory health and accident insurance systems.
*   **Public Safety:** Protecting the public from communicable diseases (e.g., managing the COVID-19 pandemic) and regulating chemicals, radiation, and food safety.
*   **Prevention:** Running national campaigns against smoking, excessive alcohol consumption, and for healthy eating.

### Importance
The BAG ensures that the Swiss healthcare system remains one of the best in the world by setting high standards for quality and safety.
