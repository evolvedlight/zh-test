### Culture on the Lake
The Theater Spektakel is one of the most important performing arts festivals in Europe.
*   **Location:** It takes place at the **Landiwiese** directly on the shore of Lake Zurich.
*   **Atmosphere:** For three weeks, the meadows are transformed into a vibrant cultural village with various theater tents, outdoor stages, and many restaurants and bars.
*   **Program:** The festival focuses on international groups, often showing innovative and avant-garde productions from the Global South and Eastern Europe that are rarely seen in regular theaters.
*   **Access:** While the main shows require tickets, the Landiwiese itself is open to everyone, making it a popular meeting place for the people of Zurich during the warm summer evenings.

It is a prime example of Zurich's openness to the world and its high investment in a diverse and high-quality cultural scene.
