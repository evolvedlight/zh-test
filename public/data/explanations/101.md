### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: One of the seven departments of the Canton of Zurich is the **Baudirektion** (Building/Construction Department).

### What the Baudirektion does:
This department is responsible for the "physical" side of the canton:
*   **Infrastructure:** Building and maintaining cantonal roads and public buildings.
*   **Environment:** Protecting nature, managing water resources, and waste management.
*   **Planning:** Spatial planning and coordinating construction across the canton.
*   **Transportation:** Overseeing public transport (ZVV) in the canton.
