### The Core Definition
The correct answer is **(b)**: The highest mountain peak in Switzerland is the **Dufourspitze**.

### Facts about the Dufourspitze
*   **Height:** **4,634 meters** above sea level.
*   **Location:** It is part of the **Monte Rosa** massif in the Canton of Valais, on the border with Italy.
*   **Name:** It is named after **Guillaume-Henri Dufour**, the Swiss general and cartographer who led the creation of the first accurate maps of Switzerland.
*   **Not the Matterhorn:** While the Matterhorn (c) is much more famous for its distinctive shape, it is only 4,478 meters high‚Äîmaking it "only" the tenth-highest peak in Switzerland.

### Climbing
The Dufourspitze is a popular but challenging destination for experienced mountaineers. It was first climbed in 1855. From the top, you have an incredible view over the entire Alpine chain and down into the Po Valley in Italy.
